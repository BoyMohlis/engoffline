Online Practice - Exercises
Exercise 1

Choose the correct answer.

1 I love _________  when you treat me like that.
  A. it B. this C. there
2 Thank you -_________  is my pleasure.
  A. this B. there C. It
3 _________ is said that about 12 people died in the storm.
  A. There B. This C. It
4 _________  is no smoke without fire.
  A. There B. it C. this
5 _________ is believed that he will be set free.
  A. This B. There C. It
6 ________ is no use in asking her advice.
  A. There B. It C. This
7 _________ were thought to be over 100,000 people using public transportation every day last year.
  A. There B. It C. This
8 Bungee jumping is amazing! I was totally shocked when I did _________  the first time.
  A. this B. it C. there

Exercise 2

Rewrite each sentence so that it has a similar meaning, using the word in parentheses.

  1. There is no point in cheating on the final exam. (worth)
  2. I spent two days completing the essay. (take)
  3. It’s your birthday. I will buy you a drink. (because)
  4. Today is a perfect day. We should go to the beach. (it)
  5.  I come back to the classroom but I didn’t see my friends. (anybody)
  6. You should be careful when you buy something online. (idea)
  7. I don’t have enough time to finish my painting. (there)

Exercise 3

Complete the sentences with ‘it’ or ‘there’.

  1. ______ is difficult to find a key in the dark.
  2. ______ are more people living in the city than I thought.
  3. ______ is the oldest house in my city.
  4. Watch your step. ______ isn’t any hurry.
  5. ______ used to be a lake here before.
  6. ______ is time for you to go to bed.
  7. If ______ is a fight between your two favorite heroes, which one would you root for?
  8. You should be ______ at 5!

Exercise 4

Rewrite each sentences so that it has a similar meaning, using the word in parentheses.

  1. They will lose this game. (no question)
  2. It’s the only way. (there)
  3. You don’t have to say anything. (nothing)
  4. It is pity if you fail your exam. (shame)
  5. You should go to bed early. (no good)
  6. I had a small problem playing that puzzle game. (a bit difficult)
  7. I didn’t leave any apples for him. (no)

Exercise 5

Complete the conversation with “it”,  “there” or “that”.

Lacy: It’s Lacy, who’s calling please?
Samatha: Hi Lacy, (1) _____ is me, Sam. I want to invite you to my housewarming party next Monday. My address is 271 Lee St., Apartment number 282.
Lacy: Oh Sammy! You finally bought an apartment of your own! (2) _____ is really good to hear. Congratulations! How long does (3) _____ take to get there from the bus stop?
Samatha: Just a few minutes. You can drive there if you want.
Lacy: Ok, is (4) _____ free parking at your building?
Samatha: Yes, (5) _____ is just to the left of my building so you can find (6) _____ easily.
Lacy: Ok, I will bring you the perfect housewarming gift. See you (7) _____ next Monday.
Samatha: Oh, thank you, but (8) _____ is no need to bring a gift. Goodbye.

Answers

Ex. 1

1. A 5. C
2. C 6. A
3. C 7. A
4. A 8. B

Ex. 2

1. It’s not worth cheating on the final exam. 5. There wasn’t anybody in the classroom when I came back.
2. It took me two days to complete the essays. 6. It’s a good idea to be careful buying online.
3. Because it’s your birthday, I will buy you a drink. 7. There is not enough time for me to finish my painting.
4. It’s a perfect day to go to the beach.  

Ex. 3

1.  It 5. There
2. There 6. It
3. It 7. there
4. There 8. there

Ex. 4

1. There is no question that they will lose this game. 5. It is not good going to bed late.
2. There is no other way. 6. It is a bit difficult to play that puzzle game.
3. There is nothing you have to say. 7. There are no apples left for him.
4. It is a shame if you fail your exam.  

Ex. 5

1. it 5. it
2. That 6. it
3. it 7. there
4. there 8. there
