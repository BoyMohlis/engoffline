Day 96 - Rapid Response - In a relationship

Step 1: Practice catch-up speaking at native speed

✔️ Practice catch-up speaking along with Recording 16.6 from 3 to 5 times. Use the transcript and a dictionary if necessary.

Recording 16.6 - Perfection - Native Speech

Lesson 16 A happy ending

Recording 16.6 - Perfection - Native Speech

Topic 1: The Answers

Mary – Kate’s been gone for a full week. I really miss her, James.

Kate – I’m back!

James – Kate! What a nice surprise!!

Mary – We´ve all been really looking forward to this moment.

James – Right. So how was your trip? Did the time away help you with your decisions?

Mary – James couldn’t do almost anything without you here, Kate!

James – Mary! You´re exaggerating again…

Kate – Okay, well it’s true; I´ve done a lot of thinking. James, I did have some head space and a chance to chew things over.

James – And? Where did all this chewing leave you?

Kate – I’ve come to a final decision. The answer is ‘yes’. I accept. There’s no doubt really… I should give it a go… I need management experience… And besides, what’s the worst thing that can happen?

James – Oh Kate. So you were thinking only about work? Don´t you remember the other, more important decision?! Something specifically related to you and me?!

 

Topic 2: It Just Doesn’t Feel Right

Kate – Look, uh, James. There’s no good way to say this to you.

James – Oh, no!

Kate – I just think, we both could use some space right now. It’s not you, it’s me. I’m just really trying to focus on my career right now, you know.

James – I know, Kate. But it doesn’t mean you have to forget about your personal life.

Kate – I know this hurts, but you deserve someone better. I just hope we can still be friends.

James – So, it’s a no, huh?

Kate – Yes, it´s a no.

Mary – Oh, Kate. It’s not my place to interfere, but James is really a wonderful guy.

Kate – That’s right, Mary. James, you are smart, sincere, good-looking, and all the other girls would like to be with you.

James – You don’t have to sugar-coat it.

Kate – But that’s the truth. I´m trying to tell it to you straight.

James – Kate, I’ve made it very clear what I think about you and your answer. It´s just so unexpected. I think I need my time away.

Kate – What do you mean? You can’t possibly leave E+ Education!

James – I’m sorry. I quit effected today. This has left a really bad taste in my mouth.

Kate – James, please, don’t be too hasty. Let’s talk about this. We can work something out, just don’t rush into making a decision, please. You’re my best, my only, content developer. Try to separate your business from your personal life.

James – Sorry Kate, I just need to be away from you for some time.

 

Topic 3: This Is The Final Call

Mary – Kate, are you OK?

Kate – Yes, I suppose so. But something’s just not right.

Mary – Well, you’ve lost a valuable member of your team.

Kate – I know, and a good friend, too.

Kate – Excuse me everyone, could we gather round? Mary, I would like to advertise for a replacement for James today. And I wanna make some changes in the office. I need to turn this business around and make it the world’s leading company and I’m ready to hear your ideas.

Mary – Is everything all right?

Kate – I can’t stop thinking about James. He seemed so upset when he left and it’s not the same without him around. He taught me a lot, and made me laugh all the time.

Mary – Kate, it’s time to swallow your pride!

Kate – Swallow what?

Mary – Admit you’re wrong, be honest with yourself and follow your heart, go and get him back!

Kate – You’re right! I really must call him, find out where he is, and make sure he´s all right.

Mary – He must be at the airport by now. He told me he’d make a fresh start in Brazil. Hang out at the beach, drink some cold beer, and watch a football game or two…

Kate – I’ve got to get to the airport quickly before James gets on that flight. Taxi! Taxi!

 

Topic 4: In A Relationship

Kate – Thanks! Oh, excuse me, do you know where the check-in desk for flights to São Paulo is?!

Man – Yeah, over there, go straight and then turn left.

Kate – Thanks a lot.

Kate – James, James! Wait!

James – Kate? How… What… What are you doing here?

Kate – Don’t go… Please… We need you… I need you.

James – But I don’t understand… You flat out rejected me. You dumped me in front of everyone!

Kate – Yes but… I had second thoughts. James… You’re really a wonderful guy and… I don’t want you to leave, and I think I love you. I’m head over heels for you!

James – Yes! I won’t be needing these anymore.

Step 2: Practice rapid response

✔️ Without the transcript, listen to Recording 16.10 and give short answers from 1 to 2 times.

✔️ Use the transcript and practice responding as instructed in the transcript from 1 to 3 times.

✔️ Practice listening and answering to Recording 16.10 from 3 to 5 times until you feel confident in giving full-sentence responses.

Part A: Building Comprehension

Why does Kate go to the airport?

Kate is on her way to the airport to try and catch James before he boards his flight to South America.

Because she tries to catch James before he boards his flight to South America.

She goes to the airport to try and catch James before he flies to South America.

How does Kate ask for direction to the check-in desk?

“Excuse me, do you know where the check-in desk for flights to São Paulo is?”

She says: “Excuse me, do you know where the check-in desk for flights to São Paulo is?”

She asks: “Excuse me, do you know where the check-in desk for flights to São Paulo is?”

Is James surprised to see Kate at the airport?

Yes, he is. He asks her: “How…what…what are you doing here?”

James is surprised to see Kate at the airport. He wonders what´s she doing there.

He’s really surprised when seeing Kate at the airport. He says: “How…what…what are you doing here?”

What does Kate say to ask James not to go?

“Don’t go…please…we need you…I need you.”

She tells him not to go because she needs him.

She says, “Don’t go…please…we need you…I need you.”

Does James get confused when Kate says she needs him?

Yes, he does. He says “But I don’t understand…”

He says that he doesn´t understand.

James gets confused and says he does not understand.

Did James feel ashamed when Kate rejected him?

Yes, he did. He says “You dumped me in front of everyone!”

James felt ashamed when Kate rejected him, he says Kate dumped him in front of everyone.

He says she dumped him in front of everyone, so he felt ashamed.

Who had second thoughts?

Kate says she had second thoughts.

Kate had second thoughts.

It’s Kate who says “I had second thoughts”.

Does Kate find it easy to explain her changes to James?

No, she doesn’t. She says she doesn’t know what to say and she´s really in a pickle.

Kate says, “I don’t know what to say. I´m really in a pickle.”

She doesn’t know what to say, and she´s really in a pickle. She finds it isn’t easy to explain her changes to James.

Does Kate say she loves James?

Yes, she does. She says, “I think I love you. I´m head over heels for you!”

She says she loves him. She´s head over heels for him.

She loves him. She´s head over heels for him.

Does James fly to South America?

No, he doesn’t. He rips up his airline tickets and says, “I won’t be needing these anymore”

He says he won’t be needing his airline tickets anymore.

James rips up his airline tickets and says he won’t be needing them anymore.

Part B: In Other Words

We need somebody to rescue us in this tough negotiation.

We need somebody to save the day in this tough negotiation.

Why do you think some clients are turning us down like this?

Why do you think some clients are flat out rejecting us like this?

It certainly is a terrible feeling when somebody rejects you rudely in a relationship.

It certainly is a terrible feeling when somebody dumps you in a relationship.

She reconsidered the possibility of enrolling in our excellent English course.

She had second thoughts about the possibility of enrolling in our excellent English course.

You should consider it carefully whenever somebody offers you something that´s too good to be true.

You should do a lot of thinking whenever somebody offers you something that´s too good to be true.

I found myself in a difficult situation when I couldn´t understand the client´s English!

I found myself in a pickle when I couldn´t understand the client´s English!

When you fall in love, you can do some strange things.

When you´re head over heels for someone, you can do some strange things.

Part C: Language to Go – How to Keep Somebody By Your Side

What could you say to a special person to tell them you’re reconsidering your relationship?

I´ve been doing a lot of thinking.

How could you let them know you actually missed them?

I didn´t know how much I missed you until you´d gone.

How could you say you´ve had second thoughts?

I´ve changed my mind about us.

What could you say to that person to let them know you´re unsure of your feelings?

I don´t know what to say, I´m really in a pickle.

How could you tell that special person that you´re really crazy for them?

I´m head over heels for you!

What do we say when we hope that a couple has a happy ending?

Now the sun can set on the happy couple.
