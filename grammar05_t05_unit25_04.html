Exercise 1

The following sentences use incorrect words. Change them into nouns using the appropriate suffixes.

  1. Violence is not the best solve to settle an argument.
  2. Not long ago, I read a popular artion.
  3. You’re old enough to take responsible for what you have done.
  4. Mike can’t make a decide about his vacation.
  5. Teachers always find a way to motivate students to pay attend to their lectures.
  6. His achieve was partly due to the support he received from his family.
  7. His mom told him to buy some medical.
  8. Our world has become modern because of industrialy.

Exercise 2

Match the underlined words in 1-10 to explanations A-J.

1. She is a pianist A. An aquatic sport.
2. Patriotism is one of the good characteristics of Vietnamese people. B. Using violence and extreme actions to threaten a country or operation.
3. Tom and Jake have a close friendship. C. A daughter of a king.
4. Terrorism is the most worrisome issue nowadays. D. The person plays piano.
5. Teacher is not a popular job. E.  The right of a person to express him or herself,  to do what he or she wants.
6. Swimming is a way to keep the body in shape. F. A strong belief in one’s country.
7. Princess Elizabeth is the most beautiful woman in the world. G. A device  created by a person.
8. I need your final decision. H. A person who transfers his knowledge to other people.
9. Freedom is what the Vietnamese people needed before their independence in 1975. I. A relationship between friends.
10. Einstein’s invention helped the human race take  steps toward a better life. J. A conclusion or resolution  that appears after careful thought.

7. _______ is the best way to improve your skills.
  a. Train b. Trainee c. Training d. Trainingly
8. ________ was a good time for many people.
  a. Childhood b. Child c. Children d. Childlike

Exercise 5

Use the prompts to make sentences.

  1. I / must / manage / the employ.
  2. The apply / accept / by the CEO.
  3. Many people / believe / God / human’s create.
  4. They / get / satisfy / be successful in life.
  5. It / bad / parents / make /  compare / between children.
  6. She / wear down / poor and ill.
  7. This / greatest / scientific achieve / century.
  8. Toys / necessary / the mental grow / children.

2. artion => article 6. achieve => achievement
3. responsible => responsibility 7. medical => medicine
4. decide => decision 8. industrialy => industrialization

2. F 7. C
3. I 8. J
4. B 9. E
5. H 10. G

4. a 8. a

Ex. 4

3. swimming 7. leadership
4. development 8. ability

Ex. 5
