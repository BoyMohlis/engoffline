Online Practice - Exercises
Exercise 1

Underline the correct word and add commas where necessary.

  1. I don’t understand what / which he just said.
  2. The woman who / that is sitting next to me is very beautiful.
  3. I’m talking with the man who / which is a successful businessman.
  4. I traveled to Singapore that / which was a beautiful place.
  5. The bed, that / which I sleep on, is very comfortable.
  6. Peter has just married Jane what / which is wonderful.
  7. Hanoi that / which is the capital of Vietnam attracts many tourists.
  8. She doesn’t need anything that / what he gives her.

Exercise 2 & 3

Exercise 2: Join the pairs of sentences. Use commas where necessary.

  1. The man is standing over there. He is my boss.
  2. This movie is really great. I saw it last night.
  3. Charlie sings very well. He is American.
  4. John gives me a box. It has a cat inside.
  5. Can you open the mail? I sent it to you yesterday.
  6. He went to university. It made his parents happy.
  7. Justin is my brother. He is very smart.
  8. Swimming is an exercise. It can release stress.
  9. I have just moved to London. My parents lived there ten years ago.
  10. It is spring.  It’s the season when cherry blossoms bloom.

Exercise 3: Are these sentences right or wrong? Correct those which are wrong.

  1. She loves someone, whom she’s never seen.
  2. I’ve just bought a car who is very expensive.
  3. My sister, who is still in high school has a job that pays well.
  4. Do you remember the man we met last week?
  5. The woman whom I love the most is my mother.
  6. They are talking about the girl who hobby is photography.
  7. She lent me the book she read yesterday.
  8. Do you know the reason which they broke up?

Exercise 4

Choose the correct option.

1. I don’t  understand __________.
A. what about he was talking B. about what he was talking C. what he was talking about.
2. Is that the company __________ you work?
A. for which B. for that C. for who
3. I know the man __________ are great.
A. who the ideas B. whose ideas C. the ideas of who
4. A pair of glasses is __________ you need when you are nearsighted.
A. what B. which C. that
5. Hanoi is the place __________ I was born and raised
A. when B. where C. why
6. I have a selfie with this man, but I don’t remember _______ he is.
A. who B. which C. —
7. “One Piece”, __________, has some new chapters.
A. that I interested in B. I interested in which C. which I am interested in
8. Mr. Beans has three daughters, all of __________ were engaged at the same time.
A. which B. whom C. who
9. She broke up with him, __________ surprises me somewhat  .
A. that B. what C. which
10. Let me know  __________ you arrive.
A. when B. where C. why

Exercise 5

Complete the letter by putting relatives clauses a-f in the correct places.

Dear Sylvia,

I’m glad to tell you that I’ve moved to New Zealand, (1) ______________________! Do you remember Danny Smith, (2)______________________? I met him yesterday. He was twenty two (3) ______________________ his photography business. Now he is the CEO  of his own company and has a very high income. Danny also shared his practical experience with me. He told me that taking advantage of  opportunities is the real reason (4) _____________________. Now I’m working at  his company. The people (5) ______________________ are very skillful. I really have to thank you, (6) ______________________ that I should start making my dreams become a reality! I’m so grateful. Keep in touch.

See you soon.

Mario

a. when he first started up
b. why he was able to become successful
c. whose photos are usually published in all the newspapers
d. with whom I work
e. where I always wanted to live
f. whose idea it was


Exercise 6

Complete the sentences with who, which or that. (More than one relative pronoun is possible in each sentences.) Then put brackets around the pronouns which can be left out.

  1. That is the man _________ loved Andy.
  2. Ronaldo is the football player _________ I like the most.
  3. There are a lot of people _________  are attending this meeting.
  4. He can do anything _________  he wants.
  5. The man_________  talked to you is my father.
  6. I know the novel _________ the film based on.
  7. Harry Potter is the book _________ was written by J. K. Rowling.
  8. There’s a lot of places _________  humans never go.
  9. Jenny has studied very hard. That is the reason _________ she got the scholarship to the university of her dream.
  10. We are looking at the boy, _________ father is a billionaire.

Answers

Ex. 1

1. I don’t understand what / which he just said. 5. The bed, that / which I sleep on, is very comfortable.
2. The woman, who / that is sitting next to me, is very beautiful. 6. Peter has just married Jane, what / which is be wonderful.
3. I’m talking with the man who / which is a successful businessman. 7. Hanoi, that / which is the capital of Vietnam, attracts many tourists.
4. I traveled to Singapore, that / which was a beautiful place. 8. She doesn’t need anything that / what he gives her.

Ex. 2

1. The man (who is) standing over there is my boss. 6. He went to university which made his parents happy.
2. This movie, which I saw last night, is really great. 7. Justin, who is very smart, is my brother.
3. Charlie, who is American, sings very well. 8. Swimming, which is an exercise, can release stress.
4. John gives me a box which has a cat inside. 9. I have just moved to London, where my parents lived ten years ago.
5. Can you open the mail which I sent you yesterday? 10. Spring is the season in which / when cherry blossoms bloom.

Ex. 3

1. Correct 5.   Correct
2. I’ve just bought a car which is very expensive. 6. They are talking about the girl whose hobby is photography.
3. My sister, who is still in high school, has a job that pays well. 7. Correct
4. Correct 8. Do you know the reason why they broke up?

Ex. 4

1. C 6. A
2. A 7. C
3. B 8. B
4. A 9. C
5. B 10. A

Ex. 5

1.   e 4.   b
2.  c 5. d
3. a 6. f

Ex. 6

1.   That is the man who / that disliked Andy. 6.   I know the novel (that / which) the film based on.
2. Ronaldo is the football player (that) I like the most. 7. Harry Potter is the book (that / which) was written by J. K. Rowling.
3. There are a lot of people who / that are attending this meeting. 8. There’s a lot of places (that / which) humans never go.
4. He can do anything (which / that) he wants. 9. Jenny has studied very hard. That is the reason why she got the scholarship to the university of her dream.
5.   The man who / that talked to you is my father. 10.  We are looking at the boy,whose father is a billionaire.
