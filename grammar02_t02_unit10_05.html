Online Practice - Exercises
Exercise 1

Complete this school report about a sister written by her brother with the correct comparative or superlative form of the words in brackets. Use than wherever necessary.

There are 4 people in my family; my father, my mother, my sister and I. My (1) _______ (old) sister, Lisa is (2) _______________ (popular) girl in my hometown. She has everything you can dream about; beauty, intelligence, kindness… No one else in her class was (3) ____________(beautiful) her, and some people used to say that she was (4) ____________ (beautiful) girl in the whole town. She always had (5) _________ (high) grades, but she was always kind to everyone around her.

At the age of 18, she moved to London by herself to study. It seems like she loves London because she looks even (6) ________ (happy) now than she did at home. She is really busy but she still cares about me. She knows I’m a little (7) _______ (weak) in math than she is, so she takes the time to come back home and tutor me at least twice a week. That’s why my math grade is so much (8) ______ (good) now. She also tells me about her new best friend and how hard she is working to learn English.

She will have her final exams this month, so she’ll be even (9) _______ (busy) than usual and coming home (10) _______ (little) often than she used to. I hope she will pass all her tests and come back early because we miss her so much.


Exercise 2

Complete this exchange among the 3 friends right after a test, using the correct comparative or superlative form of the words in brackets.

Iren: How was your test?
Vivian: It didn’t go (1) ________ (good) we’d hoped.
Jonas: Yeah. This he test was (2) ________ (hard) than we thought.
Iren: But you guys will all pass the exam, right?
Vivian: Of course. But our grades might not be (3) ______________ (high) we expected.
Jonas: I don’t think I did very well. My mom was angry with me last time and I guess she will be even (4) ________ (angry) this time.
Iren: So how can I cheer you up? Do you want to go to the bar downtown?
Vivian: No, it’s too loud. We should go to the coffee shop. It’s (5) ___________ (quiet) there.
Jonas: I agree. The bar is (6) _________ (noisy) place in the town. You have to keep screaming (7) ____________ (loud) than the noise to be heard and I don’t think the food is (8) ___________ (good) it should be for the price.
Iren: So, do you want to meet at 8 or (9) _________ (early)?
Vivan: 8 is fine. Hopefully it will help us feel (10) _________(good) to relax a little.
Jonas: I hope so, too. See you guys at 8!

Exercise 4

Use the appropriate comparative or superlative form of the words in brackets to complete the sentences.

  1. She was __________ (clever) student in her class.
  2. Non-smokers are likely to live __________ (long) than smokers.
  3. The white car isn’t ___________ (expensive) as the red car.
  4. Do you know which is __________ (big) animal in the world?
  5. Due to global warming, the climate has been getting ________ (warm) and ________ (warm).
  6. This bowl of noodle is __________ (delicious) than any noodle I’ve ever had.
  7. Gold isn’t __________ (valuable) as diamond.
  8. Everyone was surprised at the result as the final winner was ___________ (likely) candidate.
  9. The ________ (hard) you try, the __________ (successful) you are going to get.
  10. After getting married and having 2 beautiful children, Peter is  _________ (happy) he has even been.

Exercise 5

Choose the correct option.

1 Turtles can live much ________ than people.
  A longer B longest C more long
2 Your cat is _______ than mine.
  A biggest B bigger C big
3 The ostrich is ___________ bird in the world.
  A quicker B the quickest C quick
4 I should aim at the ___________ target.
  A highest B highly C higher
5 Penguins live in _____________ place on earth.
  A coldest B colder C the coldest
6 You should go outside _______ often.
  A many B more C most
7 Our country is ________ than Russia.
  A warmer B warm C warmest
8 Run ______ or we will be late.
  A faster B fast C fastest
9 The more you learn about chemistry, the ________  you find in it.
  A most interesting B more interesting C interesting
10 My house is ________ to the school than Jane’s.
  A close B the closest C closer

Answers

Ex 1.

1. oldest 6. happier
2. the most popular 7. weaker
3. more beautiful than 8. better
4. the most beautiful 9. busier
5. the highest 10. less

Ex 2.

1. as well as 6. the noisiest
2. harder 7. louder
3. as high as 8. as good as
4. angrier 9. earlier
5. the most quiet/ the quietest 10. better

Ex 3.

1. clearier → more clearly 6. Correct
2. farer → farther 7. more hardly → harder
3. Correct 8. Correct
4. frequentlier → more frequently. 9. worst → worse
5. Correct 10. the most funny → the funniest

Ex 4.

1. the cleverest 6. more delicious
2. longer 7. as valuable
3. as expensive 8. the least likely
4. the biggest 9. harder / more successful
5. warmer / warmer 10. the happiest

Ex 5.

1. A 5. B
2. B 6. A
3. B 7. A
4. A 8. B
5. C 9. C
