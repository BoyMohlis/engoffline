Day 95 - Rapid Response - This is the final cal

Step 1: Practice catch-up speaking at native speed

✔️ Practice catch-up speaking to Recording 16.6 from 3-5 times. Use the transcript and a dictionary if necessary.

Recording 16.6 - Perfection - Native Speech

Lesson 16 A happy ending

Recording 16.6 - Perfection - Native Speech

Topic 1: The Answers

Mary – Kate’s been gone for a full week. I really miss her, James.

Kate – I’m back!

James – Kate! What a nice surprise!!

Mary – We´ve all been really looking forward to this moment.

James – Right. So how was your trip? Did the time away help you with your decisions?

Mary – James couldn’t do almost anything without you here, Kate!

James – Mary! You´re exaggerating again…

Kate – Okay, well it’s true; I´ve done a lot of thinking. James, I did have some head space and a chance to chew things over.

James – And? Where did all this chewing leave you?

Kate – I’ve come to a final decision. The answer is ‘yes’. I accept. There’s no doubt really… I should give it a go… I need management experience… And besides, what’s the worst thing that can happen?

James – Oh Kate. So you were thinking only about work? Don´t you remember the other, more important decision?! Something specifically related to you and me?!

 

Topic 2: It Just Doesn’t Feel Right

Kate – Look, uh, James. There’s no good way to say this to you.

James – Oh, no!

Kate – I just think, we both could use some space right now. It’s not you, it’s me. I’m just really trying to focus on my career right now, you know.

James – I know, Kate. But it doesn’t mean you have to forget about your personal life.

Kate – I know this hurts, but you deserve someone better. I just hope we can still be friends.

James – So, it’s a no, huh?

Kate – Yes, it´s a no.

Mary – Oh, Kate. It’s not my place to interfere, but James is really a wonderful guy.

Kate – That’s right, Mary. James, you are smart, sincere, good-looking, and all the other girls would like to be with you.

James – You don’t have to sugar-coat it.

Kate – But that’s the truth. I´m trying to tell it to you straight.

James – Kate, I’ve made it very clear what I think about you and your answer. It´s just so unexpected. I think I need my time away.

Kate – What do you mean? You can’t possibly leave E+ Education!

James – I’m sorry. I quit effected today. This has left a really bad taste in my mouth.

Kate – James, please, don’t be too hasty. Let’s talk about this. We can work something out, just don’t rush into making a decision, please. You’re my best, my only, content developer. Try to separate your business from your personal life.

James – Sorry Kate, I just need to be away from you for some time.

 

Topic 3: This Is The Final Call

Mary – Kate, are you OK?

Kate – Yes, I suppose so. But something’s just not right.

Mary – Well, you’ve lost a valuable member of your team.

Kate – I know, and a good friend, too.

Kate – Excuse me everyone, could we gather round? Mary, I would like to advertise for a replacement for James today. And I wanna make some changes in the office. I need to turn this business around and make it the world’s leading company and I’m ready to hear your ideas.

Mary – Is everything all right?

Kate – I can’t stop thinking about James. He seemed so upset when he left and it’s not the same without him around. He taught me a lot, and made me laugh all the time.

Mary – Kate, it’s time to swallow your pride!

Kate – Swallow what?

Mary – Admit you’re wrong, be honest with yourself and follow your heart, go and get him back!

Kate – You’re right! I really must call him, find out where he is, and make sure he´s all right.

Mary – He must be at the airport by now. He told me he’d make a fresh start in Brazil. Hang out at the beach, drink some cold beer, and watch a football game or two…

Kate – I’ve got to get to the airport quickly before James gets on that flight. Taxi! Taxi!

 

Topic 4: In A Relationship

Kate – Thanks! Oh, excuse me, do you know where the check-in desk for flights to São Paulo is?!

Man – Yeah, over there, go straight and then turn left.

Kate – Thanks a lot.

Kate – James, James! Wait!

James – Kate? How… What… What are you doing here?

Kate – Don’t go… Please… We need you… I need you.

James – But I don’t understand… You flat out rejected me. You dumped me in front of everyone!

Kate – Yes but… I had second thoughts. James… You’re really a wonderful guy and… I don’t want you to leave, and I think I love you. I’m head over heels for you!

James – Yes! I won’t be needing these anymore.

Step 2: Practice rapid response

✔️ Listen to Recording 16.9 from 1 to 2 times. Each time, try to give short answers as fast as you can.

✔️ With the transcript, practice responding as instructed in the transcript from 1 to 3 times.

✔️ Practice listening and answering to Recording 16.9 from 5 to 7 times until you feel confident in giving full-sentence responses.

Part A: Building Comprehension

Does Kate feel happy after James has left?

No, she doesn’t. She feels something’s just not right.

Kate feels something’s just not right.

She says, “Something’s just not right.”

Does Kate call for a meeting with her team?

Yes, she does.

She says: “Excuse me everyone, can we gather round?”

She asks everyone to gather round.

What does she inform her team about James?

She says, “I would like to advertise for a replacement for James today.”

She informs them that she would like to advertise for a replacement for James today.

She says that she´d like to advertise for a replacement for James today.

Does Kate forget James and turn the business around?

No, she doesn’t. She says, “I can’t stop thinking about James.”

She says she can’t stop thinking about James.

She can’t stop thinking about James.

What does Kate recall about James?

She says, “He taught me a lot, and made me laugh all the time.”

She remembers James taught her a lot, and made her laugh all the time.

She recalls how James taught her and made her laugh all the time.

Who advises Kate to follow her heart and go get James back?

Mary does when she says, “Be honest with yourself and follow your heart, go and get him back!”

Mary advises Kate to follow her heart and go get James back.

Mary tells Kate to follow her heart and go get James back.

Does Kate follow Mary’s advice?

Yes, she does. She says Mary is right.

Kate says Mary is right and she really must call James, find out where he is, and make sure he’s all right.

Kate follows Mary’s advice when she wants to call James, find out where he is, and make sure he’s all right.

Where does Mary say James must be now?

Mary says, “He must be at the airport by now”

She says James must be at the airport by now.

Mary says he must be at the airport by now.

Where will James fly to?

He told Mary he’d make a fresh start in Brazil.

James told Mary he’d like to make a fresh start in Brazil.

He will fly to Brazil.

Why do you think Kate has to go to the airport so quickly?

Because she’s afraid James will get on that flight, she goes to the airport so quickly.

She has got to get to the airport quickly before James gets on that flight.

Kate gets to the airport so quickly to stop James from getting on that flight.

Part B: In Other Words

He never really felt comfortable speaking English all day long.

He never really felt right speaking English all day long.

It´s very hard for this group to become more serious to start to work in the morning.

It´s very hard for this group to get down to business in the morning.

The boss told us we need to adjust some things starting this week.

The boss told us we need to make some changes starting this week.

Companies that lose money need to immediately find a way to make things better.

Companies that lose money need to immediately find a way to turn the business around.

My father is always eager to hear about my travel adventures.

My father is always keen to hear about my travel adventures.

Some people can never find a way to become less proud.

Some people can never find a way to swallow their pride.

When do you think we’re going to be able to start everything again after this?

When do you think we’re going to be able to make a fresh start after this?

Part C: Language to Go – How to Announce New Changes

What would you say to your team if you wanted them to be more serious?

We need to get down to business.

What could you say to make them think changes are necessary?

We need to turn the business around to make it more successful.

What could you say if you welcome new ideas from the team?

I’m keen to hear your ideas on this.

What could you say if you want to make your company into more of a market leader?

Let´s make this: the world’s leading company in our area.

How would you announce some general changes to your team?

We’re going to make some changes in the office.
