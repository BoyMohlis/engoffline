Exercise 2

Choose the words from the box to complete the conversation.

quickly thoroughly gently correctly
slowly always rarely outside

Mrs. Adam: Good evening, Joan. Sorry for being late. The dinner went well. We have (1) __________ had time for each other since we had the baby. When we realized that we were late, we drove  (2) _________ to get back in time.   
Joan: Don’t worry. I’m happy to help. Besides, I love playing with  little Jasper. I (3) _________ love playing with children.
Mrs. Adam: Thank you. Did you mix the formula (4) _________ like I told you?
Joan: Yes, I mixed it (5) _________, just like your note said. Oh, where is Mr. Adam?
Mrs. Adam: Well done. Give the baby to me (6) _________, please. He is smoking (7) _________. Oh here he is! Close the door (8) _________, honey. Our baby is sleeping. Thanks again, Joan. Good night.

Exercise 3

Choose the correct adverb to complete the text.

I was (1) hardly/ almost lost in the forest! Last month my husband and I had a short vacation in a  beautiful hotel in the forest. We wanted to walk to the hotel instead of using the hotel shuttle.  We talked to a tour guide and he told us which way to go. I (2) probably/ always pay attention to what people talk about but I was very excited and (3) completely/ hardly remembered anything! We walked around and took a lot of pictures of the forest, but after a few moments, I (4) virtually/ hardly saw anybody on the road. (5) At least/ Finally we realized we were (6) really/ completely lost because the road we chose came to an impasse. The sun was going down and I was  scared that we would have to spend all night in the forest. (7) Fairly/ Luckily, my husband remembered the tourist guide’s phone number. He called him and a hotel shuttle was sent to pick us up (8) fast/ immediately. It turned out the hotel was just 500 feet from where we were standing and we had just chosen the wrong way at the crossroad! (9) Maybe/ probably if we had paid more attention to what the tour guide  said, we wouldn’t have wasted our first day. Apart from that, we had a great vacation in the lovely hotel and enjoyed the fresh air in the woods. I hope to go back (10) here/ there another time.

Exercise 6: Rewrite each sentence so that it has a similar meaning, using the word in brackets.

  1. He is usually late. (hardly)
  2. I can’t lift the table. (enough)
  3. She is a quick learner (learn)
  4. I’m good at playing the piano. (well)
  5. I usually have classes in the afternoon. (mainly)
  6. This song is very popular with young people. (mostly)
  7. I have every soccer player’s card but Messi’s. (almost)
  8.  He finished his housework at the last minute, right before his mother came back. (finally)
  9. You will pass the exam. (perhaps)
  10. We finished our homework at the last minute. (barely)

Ex.1

1. outside 6. carefully
2. Sunday morning 7. lightly
3. inside 8. back
4. every day 9. always
5. rarely 10. at noon

Ex. 2

1. rarely 5. thoroughly
2. quickly 6. slowly
3. always 7. outside
4. correctly 8. gently

Ex. 3

1. almost 6. completely
2. always 7. Luckily
3. hardly 8. immediately
4.  hardly 9. Maybe
5. Finally 10. there

Ex. 4

1. last 5. every
2. completely 6. Maybe
3. hardly 7. on Saturday
4.  there 8. Absolutely

Ex. 5

1. e 5. b
2. f 6. h
3. a 7. d
4. c 8. g

Ex. 6

1. He is hardly ever on time. 6. This song is popular mostly with young people.
2. I’m not strong enough to lift the table. 7. I have almost every soccer player’s card but Messi’s.
3. She learns very quickly. 8. He finally finished his housework at the last minute, right before his mother came back.
4. I play the piano very well. 9. Perhaps you will pass the exam.
5. I mainly have classes in the afternoon. 10. We barely finish our homework at the last minute.
