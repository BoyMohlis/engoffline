Online Practice - Exercises
Exercise 1

Complete the sentences using “unless” or “if”

  1. _______ you study hard, you will fail the final exams.
  2. _______you want to take a  trip to Thailand, you have to prove your financial means.
  3. _______ you don’t have a  proper diet, you will have many health problems when you get older.
  4. _______ you take your dirty shoes off, you won’t be allowed to come into my room.
  5. _______ I were you, I  would break up with that boy.
  6. _______ you spoke Japanese, you could go to Japan for business.
  7. _______ you lost 10 pounds, you won’t fit into that dress.
  8. _______ you push yourself hard, you won’t succeed.
  9. You are not allowed to be here _______ you are with your parents.
  10. Call me _______ you want to hang out again.

Exercise 2

Choose the correct option, A, B, or C to complete the sentences.

1 If the sun stopped shining,the earth _____________________ in the dark .
  A would be B will be C is
2 If there weren’t any other animals else on earth other than but humans, what _____________________ to us?
  A would happen B will happen C happen
3 If you saw see a fight, what _____________________ do?
  A do B would you C will do
4 If we cut down off all the trees in the world, we _____________________.
  A die B would die C will die
5 If we have enough time, we _____________________ it.
  A could finish B can finish C finish
6 If we stop breathing, we _____________________ .
  A would die B die C will die
7 Do you think if we had  _____________________ in the 1900s we would have been happy?
  A lived B living C live
8 If you don’t find the key, we _____________________ outside all night.
  A will be B are C be

Exercise 3

Rewrite each sentences so it that the begins as shown.

  1. Wash your hands or you won’t have any cookies after lunch. (Unless)
  2. I wish I could be a manager so I would have a higher salary. (If)  
  3. If you’re not going outside, help me with the computer. (Unless)
  4. Help me with the homework if you are not busy, Mom. (Unless)
  5. If you don’t take an umbrella, you will get wet. (Take)
  6. Don’t try to lift the desk or you will get hurt. (If)

Exercise 4

Complete the conversation with a suitable form of the verb in brackets.

Fia: Hi Vivian, what’s up with that sad look?
Vivian: Hi Fia! I’m thinking about choosing a university. I have a student grant from University of Arts but my mom told me to study in the Fine Arts University in the city.
Fia: The University of Arts in  London? If I (1) ________ (am) you, I (2)  ________ (apply) to  University of Arts without even thinking about it! .
Vivian: But if I (3)  ________ (study) at  Fine Arts University, I (4)  ________ (can go) home at least twice a week.  If I (5)  ________  (go) to University of Arts, I can only (6)  ________ (go) home once a month, unless I (7)  ________ (drop)  a few classes.
Fia: It’s also a chance for you to be more independent. (8)  ________(do not) worry if you (9)  ________ (have) some problems with making  new friends or with classes. I will help you, and you can call me whenever you need to.
Vivian: You are right. But what if I (10)  ________ (cannot) keep up with everyone at University of Arts? It’s a lot different than our hometown!
Fia: If you try University of Arts and then (11)  ________ (think) that it’s too much for you, you can always (12) ________(come) back here and study with me.   Just give it a chance!
Vivian: You are right. Thanks for your advice.
Fia: You are welcome, dear.

Exercise 5

Look at these first conditional sentences. Do they refer to a probable future event or an unlikely situation? If they refer to an unlikely situation, change them to the second conditional sentences.

  1. Misa will study in Hogwarts if she has a permission letter.
  2. I will work in the company downtown if I don’t get a better offer.
  3. If I get home from school early, I will cook dinner.
  4. If I can meet the President of the US, I will be shocked to death.
  5. If I am a millionaire, I will buy a house in Beverly Hills.  
  6. If Helen calls, tell her I’m outside.
  7. If you ask me, I will choose the red dress.
  8. I will wear a sweater if it’s cold tomorrow.
