Com Fluency

Com Fluency is an advanced English course that specializes in communicative English for working professionals, beneficial to communicating in office and real-life settings.
All the lessons are set in the context of E+ Education, one of the fastest growing EdTech companies in the world.
The course is a set of 16 interesting stories about 5 interesting people.
They are:
✔️Kate Williams: The leading female protagonist. She is a new employee who has just graduated from college and is starting to pick up essential work skills and experience.
✔️David Johnson: The Director, who is a strict person and likes to enjoy his coffee.
✔️James Brown: The Content Manager, who is responsible for the content of all products at E+ Education.
✔️Mary Jones: The Secretary, who is a highly dedicated and caring person.
✔️Ted: The narrator, who gives helpful advice to Kate on how to use appropriate language in communicating.
Besides, other characters will also appear in the stories to create more diverse communicative contexts.
Each story is a daily occurrence for employees at E+ Education’s office. Throughout the course, you will see integrated into these stories are some of the most common communication topics in the English language.

1. CONVERSATION

☑️ Provides helpful advice for communicating in English and conducting appropriately in office and real-life situations through the stories of employees at E+ Education.
☑️ Enables you to listen and comprehend in all communication situations in office and real-life situations.
☑️ Saves 71% of your time and effort to practice English listening.

2. PERFECTION

☑️ Includes dialogues that have been designed based on the most common communicative topics.
☑️ Enables you to use English words and expressions fluently and flexibly.
☑️ Helps you master popular idioms, proverbs, and slang words in advanced communicative English.

3. RAPID RESPONSE

☑️ Building Comprehension: Includes questions and answers about the situations from the stories, increasing your ability to answer questions in different ways.
☑️ In Other Words: Helps you use English idioms, proverbs, and slang words fluently.
☑️ Language to Go: Improve your response abilities in practical situations.

Practice Instructions

✔️The optimal time to study each Lesson is within 1 week.
✔️In 7 days, you will spend 6 days practicing and 1 day completely resting, preparing for the next Lesson.
✔️The daily practice time is about 1-2 hours.
✔️If daily practice isn’t possible, you can spread your schedule to about 2-3 sessions/week. Then, you will need 2-3 weeks to complete 1 Lesson.
✔️We encourage you to spend at least 16 weeks to complete the course, in order to achieve English mastery like a native speaker.
