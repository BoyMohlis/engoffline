Online Practice - Exercises
Exercise 1

Complete Matthew’s email to his wife about his foreign business trip:

To my dear wife,
Thanks for the sweaters that you (1) sent/were sending last Monday. I (2) didn’t have/wasn’t having time to answer you earlier because I (3) had/was having a lot of urgent work to finish. Japan is  the most beautiful country that I have ever visited. Last Tuesday, I had the chance to visit Kyoto. The city was so breathtaking and I filled  my iPhone with pictures of it. Yesterday, I (4) hung/was hanging out with a colleague as we (5) saw/were seeing the fireworks in the sky.  The different kinds of fireworks blew my mind. At that moment, I really (6) missed/was missing you and our daughter. I will come back home soon and be with you both.
Write soon.
Matthew

Exercise 2: Choose the correct answers.

1: When I watched television in the living room,__________
A. my mom cooked dinner in the kitchen.
B. my mom was cooking dinner in the kitchen.

2: The cup broke into pieces when __________
A. it fell on the ground.
B. it was falling on the ground.

3: When I got up yesterday,___________
A. it was raining outside.
B. it rained outside.

4: Paul arrived at the train station
A. while the train was leaving the station.
B. when the train left the station.

5: We finished the exams
A. while the bell was ringing.
B. when the bell rang.

6: Max was playing games __________
A. when Tim read books downstairs.
B. while Tim was reading books downstairs.

7: Chris was on a  trip to Greece ______
A. while Tommy was working in the hospital.
B. while Tommy worked in the hospital.


Exercise 3

Rewrite the below sentences with “used to” and the verb in brackets.

1. She was a dentist. (be)
2. Before he retired, he worked hard. (work)
3. Taz played for New York after the Cavaliers fired him. (play)
4. His liver is not in good condition because he drank too much alcohol before. (drink)
5. Johnson drove a Mercedes before he traded it in for a Porsche. (drive)
6. Germany was the previous World Cup champion. (be)
7. Tom was the richest person in town until he lost everything in the fire. (be)
8. Jimmy is a security guard for a supermarket now, but he danced for Jabberwocky crew 5 years ago. (dance)

Exercise 5
Complete the description.
My friend’s name is Jamesey and he is Canadian. He (1) (move) to China in 2009 and he (2) (come) to Beijing. He joined  my company a year later and worked as the manager. He was my colleague and I first (3) (meet) him when he (4) (look)  for our office. When he (5) (ask) the receptionist for the direction, I (6)(run) by quickly because I was late. I (7) (not notice) that there was a man standing in front of me, so I ran into him. I was so embarrassed that I ran away right after I (8) (pick) up all of my documents. “Well, he will never find me here.” I whispered to myself as I hid in my office. I couldn’t believe that he (9) (be) the new employee that would be working with me! I  told him that I was very sorry for the accident and he was happy to accept my apology. After that incident, we (10) (become) good friends and we get along very well ever since.

Exercise 6

Leon is going to Asia for six months. Add these verbs in the past continuous form to the conversation:

do travel not enjoy take care look for work get off
Alex Are you going on a long trip to Asia this summer?
Leon I am! I (0) was working very hard last month, as usual, but I (1) _________ long hours in the office, day in and day out.
Alex I guess you are excited about this trip, right?
Leon That’s right. Do you want to know how I got this idea? Last Thursday, while (2) I _________ to work, I saw a leaflet on the street with some pictures of elephants. People (3) _________ on the train, so I had time to pick it up and read it.  
Alex What was on the leaflet?
Leon Well, they (4) _________ people to volunteer in South East Asia for one month.
Alex I think I know about that project. I (5) _________ the laundry the other day and saw an interview with the founder on TV. It’s called ‘Asia Protection Project’, right?  
Leon Really? Yes, you’re right. I was told that the founder (6) _________ of a sick elephant one day, and she came up with the idea of involving volunteers from all over the world to help.
Alex What a wonderful idea. Now that makes me want to go as well.
