Lesson 00 - Getting Started

To get started, you need to have a good grasp of the 3 core techniques that will make a difference and demonstrate the effectiveness of Com Fluency.
Thousands of Com Fluency learners have mastered communicative English for office settings within only 4 months by applying these 3 techniques skillfully.
I believe that it will truly be helpful for you.
Let’s discover!

#1 – Deep Listening Technique

This guide will help you use the Deep-listening Technique to practice English in a simple and effective way.
And here..
… are the specific steps of the Deep-listening Technique.
Please click on the button below to see the steps to apply the Deep-listening Technique.

The steps to apply the Deep-listening Technique

Step 1: Getting familiar with the listening audio

Please practice the listening exercise of Com Fluency without having to understand all the details. Try your best to get the gist of the story. Please do not use the transcript or dictionaries and do not try to translate the story into Vietnamese in this step.

Step 2: Understanding the content

Please use the transcript when needed and practice with the listening audio until you can understand all the content.

Step 3: Listen and feel

Without using the transcript, listen and imagine the story. Please listen until you can follow all the content of the listening audio easily. At the same time, pay attention to how the characters pronounce and talk in the audio. Note: Understanding all the content of the story is the most important goal of the Deep-listening Technique. kembali

#2 – Catch-up Speaking Technique

The catch-up speaking practice is a series of conversations extracted from the story.
You will get to practice all you want…
… with just 2 easy steps.
Please click on the button below to see the steps to apply the Catch-up Speaking Technique.

The steps to apply the Catch-up Speaking Technique

Step 1: Practice catch-up speaking at slow speed - Slow speech

Firstly, please listen to the audio without using the transcript. Pay attention to the enunciation of each word by the characters.
Next, please listen and speak quietly along the recording audio, noting both the content and intonation.
Practice catch-up speaking at slow speed until you can keep up with the slow speed of the characters and feel confident with your pronunciation
The most important goal in this step: recognizing the pronunciation, the ending sounds, and the stress of each word.

Step 2: Practice catch-up speaking at native speed - Native speech.

First and foremost, please listen to the audio without using the transcript. Pay attention to the enunciation of each word, the linking sounds, the sentence stresses and the intonation of the characters.
Next, listen and speak quietly along the recording audio, noting both the content and intonation.
Please practice catch-up speaking with the audio until you can keep up with the natural speed of the characters and feel confident with your voice.
The most important goal in this step: recognizing the linking sounds, the sentence stresses, and the intonation of the native speakers and doing catch-up speaking in a natural way. kembali

#3 – Rapid Response Technique

Each Rapid Response exercise of the Com Fluency course consists of 3 parts: (except for Lesson 01, where Topic 1 only has Building Comprehension)

1. Building Comprehension: Including 10 multi-dimensional questions about the story you have just practiced listening and catch-up speaking to.
2. In Other Words: Including 6 pairs of similar-meaning sentences. You will listen to simple sentences and reply with similar-meaning responses, using idioms that you have just practiced.
3. Language To Go: Including 6 questions or situations that you have practiced. You will need to respond appropriately to the context and with the most suitable answers.

In the Rapid Response recording audio, there will be a pause of about 6 seconds for you to give a response (answer). Then, you will hear some sample answers so you can evaluate your own answers and make adjustments for the next practice.
Note: In the first few practices of Rapid Response, you can use the transcript, analyze the questions beforehand and try to answer them before listening to the audio.
Please click on the button below to see the steps to apply the Rapid Response Technique.

The steps to apply the Rapid Response Technique
Step 1: Understanding the content of the story
Please use the Deep-listening Technique in this step to understand the entire story.
Step 2: Practicing giving short answers
You will need to give short answers in a timely manner, without having to concern too much about accuracy at this point.
Step 3: Practicing responsive listening and speaking in English

You will practice until you can give full answers immediately, easily and accurately to all the questions and the information being given.

The most important goal of this technique is to be able to give a response as quickly as possible. kembali
