Online Practice - Exercises
Exercise 1

Choose the correct forms, A, B, C to complete the sentences.

1 Your hair needs to be  _____________
  A. cutting B. cut C. been cut
2 He is believed _______________
  A. to be a doctor B. be a doctor C. being a doctor
3 I had my eyes  _____________ yesterday.
  A. be tested B.  tested C. testing
4 She is said __________ the tallest woman in town.
  A. been B. being C. to be
5 Dinosaurs were thought __________ extinct a long time ago.
  A. becoming B. to become C. to have become
6 My daughter __________  a basket of chocolates by her grandmother.
  A. was given B. give C. gave
7 He got his PSP _________.
  A. fixing B. fix C. fixed
8 The last play  ________ to be the best one  ever.
  A. expected B. is expected C. expecting
9 My dog likes ________ Don.
  A. be called B. being called C. calling

Exercise 2

Transform the following sentences into Passive Voice using the given phrases as subjects.

  1. People say that the book is very good. ⇒ It ____. / The book ____.
  2. Everyone believes that the school system needs a lot of changes. ⇒ It ____. / The school system ____. 
  3. We expect that the Vietnamese soccer team will be the Champion of this tournament. ⇒ It ____ . / The Vietnamese soccer team ____. 
  4. They thought that the boat had left the port before the storm came. ⇒ It ____. / The boat ____. 
  5. They suppose that all parents know that tomorrow is the Sport day. ⇒ It ____. / All parents ____. 
  6. The news reported that a diamond crown has been stolen last night. ⇒ It ____. / A diamond crown ____. 
  7. People rumor that the two main actors of that movie are having an affair. ⇒ It ____. / The two main actors of that movie ____. 

Exercise 3

Choose the correct option to complete the sentences below.

1. It’s rumored _____________ this neighborhood soon.
  A. that the singer will move to B. will move to C. that the singer to move to
2. Most pet dogs enjoy ______________ routinely to engage with the outside environment.
  A. being walked  B. to be walking C. to walk
3. The crown was reported ______________  stolen last night from the museum.
  A. to be B to have been C. that it was
4. I don’t like the idea of skydiving. Imagine ______________ suddenly mid-air, it’s terrifying.
  A. to drop B. to be dropped C. being dropped
5. This movie is excellent. It deserves ______________  all over the world.
  A. it being shown B. to show C. to be shown
6. The business is said ______________  more than 1 million dollars last month.
  A. to have made B. to make C. that it’s made
7. Did she want her ring ______________ of 24 carat diamond?
  A that is made B. to make C. to be made
8. A new public transport system is expected ______________  in place by 2020.
  A. to be B. to have been C. that is
9. It is believed ______________ married in April next year.
  A. them to be B. that they will be C. that they are

Exercise 4

Choose the correct form to complete the text.

Josh is my family’s beloved dog. We (1) _______  when I was 14, so he is so old now. When he was small, he was believed (2) _______ the smartest dog in the village and many prizes (3) ______ to him over the years. But after that his health got worse and we stopped entering him into contests. That is why we have to (4) _______ every weekend. He is a kind dog, he loves sunbathing and having his hair (5) gently ______. He also (6) _______ with us around the park every night after dinner. Last year, he had a fever so high that he was (7) ________ ! In the morning he was all better again. It was like a miracle! For the last few months, his kennel has had some problems so I (8)  ______ . It seems that Josh hasn’t gotten used to his new kennel and he barks every night until we let him into the house. I hope he adjusts soon.
1 A have adopted him B had  adopted him C adopted him
2 A to be B is C being
3 A given B gave C were given
4 A have him checked B had him check C check
5 A brushed B brush C brushing
6 A enjoy to walk B enjoys walking C enjoy to be walked
7 A thought dying B thought to die C thought to be dead
8 A had rebuilt B had it rebuilt C have it rebuilt

1. B 6. A

4. C 9. B

 6. It was reported that a diamond crown has been stolen last night.

 7. It is rumored that the two main actors of that movie are having an affair.
