Online Practice - Exercises
Exercise 1

Choose the correct option.

  1. On his way back home, he stopped at the newsstand  to buy / buying a newspaper.
  2. He quit  to smoke / smoking because he had lung cancer.
  3. Don’t forget to turn off / turning off the light when you go to bed.
  4. I’ll never forget to date / dating her.
  5. I remember to meet / meeting you somewhere, but I can’t remember  your name.
  6. Remember to send / sending her flowers because today is her birthday.
  7. I need to wash / washing my car.
  8. My car needs to wash / washing.
  9. I love to play / playing soccer with my friend.
  10. I would love to earn / earning more money.

Exercise 2

Banner is going to go back to his hometown for a visit. He is writing a letter to  one of his friends there, Tony. Read his letter and complete the sentences using the verbs given.

Dear Tony,

I ______________ (1) (want / thank) you very much for your letter. I’m sorry to hear that you’ve lost your part time job. Don’t worry, I’m sure  you can easily find another one . Last week, I was very busy. I ______________ (2) (try / work )  extra hard to pass the final exam.

I ______________ (3) (plan/come) back home in  June, and I would ______________ (4) (like / visit) you for a few days. I ______________ (5) (try / call ) Chris and Bruce from high school  last week, but they didn’t answer . Can you please ______________ (6) (remember / tell) them that I hope to see them when I come back?

How is your little brother? I remember that he always loves candy. I’m bringing him some licorice, but if you think he would ______________ (7) (prefer / eat) some chocolate instead, I can bring him some. .

I’m looking ______________ (8) (forward / see) you!

Banner.


Exercise 3

Kate and Jay are talking about their work. Complete their conservation with the correct form of the verbs from the box.

swimming to release doing to go to practice
to have getting telling drawing to help
Kate: Hi Jay, You look really tired. Long night?
Jay: Well, I spent all night finishing my homework. (1) ___________ all these extra exercises makes me crazy.
Kate: Why did the teacher give you so much homework?
Jay: I’m not very good at (2) ___________. I’m afraid of (3) ___________ a bad grade, so I asked my Graphic Design teacher (4) ___________ me.  He gave me all these extra drawing exercises to do.
Kate: You’re lucky (5) ___________ him.  Have your skills been improving?
Jay: Just a little bit. I still need (6) ___________ more.
Kate: Do you want to go swimming with me? Maybe it will help you  (7) ___________ some of that stress!
Jay: Yes, I love  (8) ___________! Which swimming pool do you want (9) ___________ to?
Kate: How about the swimming pool near the bus station? I remembered (10) ___________ you about it last week.
Jay: Great! Let’s go.

Exercise 4 & 5

Exercise 4: Rewrite each sentence so that it begin with an -ing form as subject.

  1. It is interesting to learn a new skill.
  2. It is impossible to live on the moon.
  3.  It is very hard to finish the work on time.
  4.  It takes 4 hours to fly from Hanoi to Singapore.
  5.  It is great to work as a team.
  6. It can be difficult to get up early.
  7.  It is wrong to cheat on the exam.
  8.  It can be good for our health to swim every day.
  9. It can be tiring to climb up the mountain.
  10.  It is great to see you here.

Exercise 5: Underline the correct word.

  1. The doctor consider / mean / suggests eating breakfast before going to work.
  2. He looks overweight. He should come / involve / keep swimming with me every day.
  3. Everyone was very afraid / excited / good about going to New York.
  4. I’m not used / interested / accused to staying up late.
  5. It’s easy to pass / passing the test.
  6. No walk / walking / to walk on the grass.
  7. She must apologize to / for / of arriving late.
  8. He looks pleased to see / seeing me.
  9. I can’t stand walking / to walk to school.
  10. My mom doesn’t let me playing / to play / play near the river.

Answers

Ex. 1

1. to buy 6. to send
2. smoking 7. to wash
3. to turn off 8. washing
4. dating 9. to play or playing
5. meeting 10.  to earn

Ex. 2

1. want to thank 5. tried to call
2. tried to work 6. remember to tell
3. plan to come 7. prefer to eat
4. like to visit 8. forward to seeing

Ex. 3

1. doing 6. to practice
2. drawing 7. release
3. getting 8. swimming
4. to help 9. to go
5. to have 10. telling

Ex. 4

1. Learning a new skill is interesting. 6. Getting up early can be difficult.
2. Living on the moon is impossible. 7. Cheating on the exam is wrong.
3. Finishing the work on time is very hard. 8. Swimming every day can be good for our health.
4. Flying from Hanoi to Singapore takes 2 hours. 9. Climbing up the mountain can be tiring.
5. Working as a team is great. 10. Seeing you here is great.

Ex. 5

1. suggests 6. walking
2. come 7. for
3. excited 8. to see
4. used 9. walking
5. to pass 10. play
