Recording 16.1 - Conversation - The Answers

Lesson 16 A happy ending

Recording 16.1 - Conversation - The Answers

Narrator – Here we are again at a critical time for E+ Education where David has stepped aside and offered Kate his position. In the meantime, James has also popped the question and asked her to be his girlfriend. She’s taken a short time away to make up her mind, and we welcome her back today. 

Mary – Kate´s been gone for a full week. I really miss her, James. 

Kate – I’m back! 

James – Kate! What a nice surprise!! 

Mary – We´ve all been really looking forward to this moment. 

James – Right. So how was your trip? Did the time away help you with your decisions? 

Mary – James couldn’t do almost anything without you here, Kate! 

James – Mary! You´re exaggerating again… 

Narrator – Kate, tell them you’ve been chewing things over, which means you’ve been thinking about the proposals facing you, and say that you´ve come to a final decision. 

Kate – Okay, well it’s true; I´ve done a lot of thinking. James, I did have some head space and a chance to chew things over. 

James – And? Where did all this chewing leave you? 

Kate – I’ve come to a final decision. The answer is ‘yes’. I accept. There’s no doubt really. I should give it a chance. I need management experience. And besides, what’s the worst thing that can happen? 

James – Oh Kate. So you were thinking only about work? Don´t you remember the other more important decision?! Something specifically related to you and me?! 

Narrator – Well, Kate, he´s actually asking about the other decision that involves the two of you. How´s she going to answer that? Decisions, decisions… Let’s find out in the next topic. Now it’s time for you to practice. Enjoy!


Recording 16.2 - Conversation - It Just Doesn't Feel Right

Lesson 16 A happy ending

Recording 16.2 - Conversation - It Just Doesn't Feel Right

Narrator – Here we are again at E+ Education, all excited to know about Kate’s two tough decisions. What´s her answer primarily to James? 

Kate – Look, err, James. There’s no good way to say this to you. 

James – Oh, no! 

Kate – I just think, um, we both could use some space right now. It’s not you, it’s me. I’m just really trying to focus on my career right now, you know. 

James – I know, Kate. But it doesn’t mean you have to forget about your personal life. 

Kate – I know this hurts, but you deserve someone better. I just hope we can still be friends.

James – So, it’s a no, huh? 

Kate – Yes, it´s a no. 

Mary – Oh, Kate. It’s not my place to interfere, but James is really a wonderful guy. 

Kate – That’s right, Mary. James, you are smart, sincere, good-looking, and all the other girls would like to be with you. 

James – You don’t have to sugar-coat it. 

Kate – But that’s the truth. I´m trying to tell it to you straight. 

James – Kate, I’ve made it very clear what I think about you and your answer… it´s just so unexpected. I think I need my time away.

Kate – What do you mean? You can’t possibly leave E+ Education! 

James – I’m sorry. I quit, effected today. This’s left a really bad taste in my mouth. 

Narrator – Oh….This is serious, Kate. Maybe you need to calm him down, and make him change his mind. Tell him ‘not to be so hasty’, ‘don’t rush into a decision’ and say ‘we can work this out. You can do this, can’t you? 

Kate – I think I can. James, please, don’t be too hasty. Let’s talk about this. We can work something out, just don’t rush into making a decision, please. Y…You’re my best-my only-content developer. Try to separate your business from your personal life. 

James – Sorry Kate. I just need to be away from you for some time. 

Narrator – Now James is leaving, he´s terribly upset. What does that mean for E+ Education and Kate? Let’s find out in the next topic. Now it’s time for you to practice. Enjoy!


Recording 16.3 - Conversation - This Is The Final Call

Lesson 16 A happy ending

Recording 16.3 - Conversation - This Is The Final Call

Narrator – Welcome back to E+ Education where things are a bit strange after James left. Let’s see how everyone´s doing. 

Mary – Kate, are you OK? 

Kate – Yes, I suppose so. But something’s just not right. 

Mary – Well, you’ve lost a valuable member of your team. 

Kate – I know, and a good friend, too. 

Narrator – Well, Kate, you’re the boss now, it’s time to deal with the situation and get down to business. Tell the team you’ll find a replacement for James, and you’re also going to make some changes in the office. You could also say you want to turn the business around and make it really successful.

Kate – Okay, thanks. Excuse me everyone, could we gather round? Mary, I would like to advertise for a replacement for James today. And I wanna make some changes in the office. I need to turn this business around and …make it the world’s leading company and…I’m…I’m…ready to hear your ideas. 

Mary – Is everything all right?

Kate – I can’t stop thinking about James. He seemed so upset when he left and it’s not the same without him around. He taught me a lot, and made me laugh all the time. 

Mary – Kate, it’s time to swallow your pride! 

Kate – Swallow what? 

Mary – Admit you´re wrong, be honest with yourself and follow your heart, go and get him back! 

Kate – You’re right! I really must call him, find out where he is, and make sure he´s all right. 

Mary – He must be at the airport by now. He told me he´d make a fresh start in Brazil, hang out at the beach, drink some cold beer, and watch a football game or two… 

Kate – I’ve got to get to the airport quickly before James gets on that flight. Taxi! Taxi! 

Narrator – This is going to be really interesting! Can Kate do this deal at the last minute?!


Recording 16.4 - Conversation - In A Relationship

Lesson 16 A happy ending

Recording 16.4 - Conversation - In A Relationship

Narrator – Hello and welcome back to E+ Education where things have gotten rather dramatic all of a sudden. Kate is on her way to the airport to try and catch James before he boards his flight to South America and his new life on the other side of the world. But will she get there in time? Can she save the day once again? 

Kate – Thanks! Oh, excuse me, do you know where the check-in desk for flights to São Paulo is?! 

Man – Yeah, over there, go straight and then turn left. 

Kate – Thanks a lot. Oh, James, James! Wait! 

James – Kate? Kate! How…what…what are you doing here? 

Kate – Don’t go…please…we need you. I need you. 

James – But I don’t understand. You flat out rejected me. You dumped me in front of everyone! 

Kate – Yes but… but… I had second thoughts. 

Narrator – But what, Kate? What are you really trying to say? How can you tell him you were wrong, and you’ve changed your mind? 

Kate – Hmm, I don’t know what to say. I´m really in a pickle. 

Narrator – Tell him, “I’ve been doing a lot of thinking.” Say, “I didn’t realize how much I missed you until you´d gone.” And tell him, “I’ve changed my mind about us.” 

Kate – James… you’re really a wonderful guy and… I don´t want you to leave, and I think I love you. I´m head over heels for you! 

James – Yes! I won’t be needing these anymore. 

Narrator – Hooray. At last! We got there in the end: They´re finally in a special relationship. Now the sun can set on the happy couple. We hope you’ve been enjoying this journey with us. Keep practicing and see you soon in our next program!
