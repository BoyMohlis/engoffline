Exercise 1

Choose the correct option.

1. Deforestation always ___dangers the lives of a large number of species.
  a. in b. ous c. en d. on
2. My aunt’s house is very ___convenient.
  a. under b. dis c. in d. un
3. He is ___like his father because his father looks different from him.
  a. im b. ir c. ous d. un
4. My cousin never keeps his promises. She is an ___reliable person.  
  a. un b. able c. re d. pre
5. The younger generation is more creat____ than the older one.  
  a. able b. ive c. ous d. ful
6. Jimmy is the new champion because he ____played his competitor.
  a. post b. co c. semi d. out
7. Hanoi is famous for many of its histor____ sites.
  a. ent b. ic c. al d. fish
8. Tourists are always interested in discovering other countries’ tradition____ customs.
  a. al b. in c. ant d. ent

Exercise 2

Fill in the blanks with correct form of the word.

  1. Filmmakers always try to show viewers the different lifestyles and __________ traditions of the world. (culture)
  2. Women nowadays have more  __________ to participate in social activities. (free)
  3. “Hoang Le Nhat Thong Chi” is a  __________ novel. (history)
  4. He has a  __________ for English novels. (prefer)
  5. SAI GON GIAI PHONG is published seven days a week. It is a  __________ newspaper. (day)
  6. If you burn the garbage, it will give off a  __________ gas. (harm)
  7. Some children suffer from having an  __________ slow heart rate. (normal)
  8. He is a  __________ athlete  in spite of his old age. (wonder)
  9. The company is very efficient and offers  __________ delivery. (speed)
  10. When did  __________ come to the village? (electric)

Those games contain a lot of violence and are (4) (suit) ______________ for the mental development of youngsters. Although it’s (5)(deny) ______________that some of them may help develop children’s imagination and (6) (logic) ______________thinking skills, most games contain violent scenes. They can encourage children to lose control and their behavior often becomes more violent and (7) (danger)______________. Furthermore, a number of games charge players money for game enhancements . That leads to another problem:  Many kids try to find money so that they can pay for a better gaming experience. Some will even lie to their parents or steal from those around them. Exercise 5

Match the similar meaning pairs.

1. This dog is harmless.   A. He is disabled.
2. He is always suspicious. B. He is very nervous.
3. That man has no place to go. C. He made her upset.
4. That poor kid has only one arm. D. He was very famous.
5. He looks very stressed. E. He always misunderstands me.
6. Jimmy used to have some fame. F. She has an eye-catching look.
7. His mother was disappointed with him. G. Don’t worry, he won’t bite you.
8. She’s attractive. H. She always does everything by herself.
9. Kate is an independent woman. I. This vacation is awesome.
10. This trip is wonderful. J. He’s homeless.
