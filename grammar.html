Welcome to the journey of Grammar Essence

Getting to the heart and head of English Grammar, which is divided into 05 themes:
✔️ THEME 01 – Verb tenses (Unit 01 – Unit 07): to tell you about how English verbs function to convey meaning
✔️ THEME 02 – Parts of speech (Unit 08 – Unit 14): to explain other types of words, their connection and transformation
✔️ THEME 03 – Connecting clauses (Unit 15 – Unit 16): to help you write better and more meaningful sentences
✔️ THEME 04 – Sentence structures (Unit 17 – Unit 22): to bring a wide range of grammar structures for you to express your ideas
✔️ THEME 05 – The Additional (Unit 23 – Unit 25): where you are equipped with even more
We hope you enjoy it and “get to the heart and head of English Grammar” for your future success.
