Online Practice - Exercises
Exercise 1

Rewrite the sentences.

  1. He is running the new  restaurant.
  2. The secretary opens the mail every morning.
  3. The teacher doesn’t allow me to take the window seat..
  4. Tom’s absence worried his teacher.
  5. They are building a new school in this town.
  6. You should finish the report right now.
  7. We will  discuss the matter in the afternoon.
  8. Are you going to mail this letter ?
  9. He has to pay the bills before the fifth of the month.
  10. No one has opened that box for the past hundred years.

Exercise 2

Complete the sentence with the correct tense and voice.

  1. Everyone (shock) ____________ by the news yesterday.
  2. Almost everyone (enjoy) ____________  the lecture last night.
  3. English (speak) ____________  all over the world.
  4. A terrible crime (commit) ____________ right here at 6 o’clock last night.
  5. Mr. Smith (teach) ____________  at this university since 1985.
  6. Nothing much (say) ____________  about the matter since last week.
  7. He (borrow) ____________  the money from her two weeks ago.
  8. A new textbook (publish) ____________  by that company next year.
  9. Everything (go) ____________  well so far. There (be) ____________   any trouble yet.
  10. Who ____ the book (write) ____________  by?

Exercise 3

Complete the sentences, using the words in the brackets and an appropriate form of the verb.

  1. I can’t go to work because _____________________________. (my bike / steal/ thief)
  2. Grum was upset after _____________________________. (he / embarrass / by his friend)
  3. John hadn’t known about the interview results earlier because _____________________________. (they / only send/ yesterday)
  4. Klaus smiles happily because _____________________________. (his project / accept/ by his boss)
  5. All the children in that building were saved  because _____________________________. (the fire / put out / by the firefighters)
  6. Brad Junior is going back home soon because _____________________________. (his test/ finish/ early)
  7. The monk has to leave before _____________________________. (his temple / collapse/ because of the earthquake)
  8. Juvenile delinquency decreases quickly because __________. (a number of laws / pass / by the government)

Exercise 4

This is a process for  recycling bottles. Put the verbs in brackets in the correct tense and voice to complete the paragraph.

The process of recycling bottles (1) __________ (divide) into three main steps. After (2) __________ (use) by the customer, they will (3) __________ (collect) by vans and (4) __________(deliver) to the factory for classification. All the bottles will (5) __________ (clean) with high-pressure water jets before (6) __________ (bring) into the incinerator. In the incinerator, they will (7) __________ (melt) down into a liquid and (8) __________ (put) into molds.  After (9) __________ (shape) by the molds, the  bottles will be filled up again and (10) __________ (transport) to markets.


Exercise 5

There is a meeting between a school principal and parents. Complete the conversation.

Principal: Hello everyone. It is a great pleasure to meet all of you here. And now, I will announce some changes in our learning system and in fees. First, let’s talk about the learning system.  Next semester, students (1)________ (will / teach) about world history instead of national history like before. Also, the exam system (2) ________ (will/ replace) and students (3) ________  (will not / test) anymore. On the other hand, all our facilities (4) ________ (will / upgrade) or _______ (replace) with high-tech equipment. For example, our computer lab equipment (5) _________ (will / replace) by 200 Dell computers and our library’s books (6) ________ (will / update) with a lot more science books and research material.

Parents: So, how about the fees next semester? ______ they (7) _______ (will / increase)?

Principal: I’m glad to tell you that  fees (8) ________ (will / decrease) next semester because our school (9) ________ (was / invest) in by the ministry of education last month. Prime Minister Smith  promised that our school (10) _________ (will / invest) in even more if we have good results next  semester.

Parents: That’s great! Our children will be studying in a good environment with an improved educational system and facilities.


Answers

Ex. 1

1. The new restaurant is being run by him. 6. The report should be finished right now.
2. The mail is opened by the secretary every morning. 7. The matter will be discussed in the afternoon.
3. I am not allowed by the teacher to take the window seat. 8. Is this letter going to be mailed?
4. Tom’s teacher was worried by his absence. 9. The bills have to be paid before the fifth of the month.
5. A new school is being built in this town. 10. That box hasn’t been opened for the past hundred years.

Ex. 2

1. was shocked 6. has been said.
2. enjoyed 7. borrowed
3. is spoken 8. will be published
4. was committed 9. has gone – has not been
5. has taught 10. is – written

Ex. 3

1. my bike was stolen by the thief 5. the fire was put out  by the firefighter
2. he was embarrassed by his friend 6. his test was finished early
3. they were only sent yesterday 7. his temple collapses because of the earthquake
4. his project has just been accepted by his boss 8. a number of laws were passed by the government

Ex. 4

1. is divided 6. being brought
2. being used 7. be melted
3. be collected 8. put
4. delivered 9. being shaped
5. be cleaned 10. transported

Ex. 5

1. will be taught 6. will be updated
2. will be replaced 7. Will ___ be increased
3. will be tested 8. will be decreased
4. will be upgraded __ replaced 9. was invested
5. will be replaced 10. will be invested
