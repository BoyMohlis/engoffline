  5. Tom’s grandfather died ______ 1977 ______ the age of 79.
  6. I might not be at home ______ the morning. Can you call ______ the afternoon ?

Exercise 4

Two people set up a schedule for household chores. Complete the conversation.

Kross: Hey man, our room looks like a mess. I think it needs to be tidied up.

Ozil: OK OK, we should make a schedule. I think each person should clean 3 days each, from Monday to Saturday. We can work together (1) _____  Sunday. Is that okay with you?

Kross: That’s okay with me. So, I think we need to have a small discussion about the schedule from Monday to Saturday.

Ozil: I study (2) _____Monday, Wednesday and Saturday (3) _____1pm. And you, Kross?

Kross: I study (4) _____Tuesday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday (5) _____the morning and afternoon.

Ozil: You study (6) _____Saturday too! Oh God, how can we set up Saturday?

Kross: My class starts (7) _____7 o’clock and I can finish all my work (8) _____ 9 o’clock. How about yours?

Ozil: My class starts (9) _____7 o’clock but I won’t be home (10) _____11 o’clock.

Kross: So, I will do the chores (11) _____Monday, Wednesday and Saturday. The other days will be yours.

Ozil: That’s fine with me.


Exercise 5

Complete the letter that Sam writes for Wade.

Dear Wade,

I was very happy to  receive your letter. I’m looking forward to seeing you (1) ______ July 13th, as you mentioned (2) ______ your previous letter. I’m writing this letter to mention  some information that I think you will need to save time and money. First, there are two kinds of transportation which  go to my house. You can go (3) ______ train or you can go (4) ______ bus. However, you should go (5) ______ train because it only takes 4 hours to go straight to my city. Otherwise, you need to take at least 5 buses to get here and it may take  a lot of extra time and money. You can go to the restroom in the train station to freshen up when you arrive. After collecting your luggage, call a taxi and tell him to drive you to Dragous Street. When you are on Dragous Street, find the white house with the iron gate (6) ______ the end of the street. Then, find room 304 (7) ______ the third floor. That’s my room. I won’t be home (8) ______ 5 pm so you can take my key in the mailbox and wait for me in my room. Have fun and enjoy your trip!

Hope to see you soon. Have a nice day.

Sincerely yours,

Samantha
